What Makes a Healthy Diet?

A healthy diet includes a variety of foods that provide essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, proteins, and healthy fats. Eating a balanced diet can help maintain a healthy weight, reduce the risk of chronic diseases, and promote overall well-being.

Essential Food Groups

Focus on including the following in your daily meals:

  • Fruits and Vegetables: Packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
  • Proteins: Include lean meats, fish, beans, and legumes for muscle repair and immune function.
  • Whole Grains: Choose whole grains like brown rice, oats, and quinoa for energy and fiber.
  • Dairy or Alternatives: Provide calcium for strong bones and teeth.
  • Healthy Fats: Incorporate healthy fats from sources like olive oil, nuts, and avocados.
